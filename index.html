Ánanda Márga is an international organization which teaches meditation and the tantra yoga lifestyle — and provides social service — all over the world.

The meditation and tantra yoga lifestyle are part of a complete, modernized system, which promotes physical, mental and spiritual health and development. Some of the teachings are as old as 7000 years, such as tantric and yogic meditation techniques, yoga postures, chanting and dancing.

The modernization clarifies and elaborates the tantric and yogic teachings with perspectives adjusted for the modern, globalized human being.

The idea of ahiḿsá, non violence, explained in a practical way, is not only about not harming harmless beings, but expanded into serving the universe, protecting all life. Therefore, Ánanda Márga practices both spirituality and service to the universe, very sincerely. Furthermore, humanity is being served in all possible ways of creating a better world, such as with environmental activism, relief work, as well as actively attempting to eradicate world problems such as starvation, poverty, suppression, oppression, repression and exploitation.

The tantric and yogic practices also serve humanity, by improving physical, mental and spiritual health and development. For the practitioners, tantric and yogic practices go hand in hand with serving the universe, since these things enhance one another. Therefore, service to the universe is a vital part of tantric and yogic practices.

The modernized philosophy also explain the tantric and yogic philosophy with scientific concepts and language. Evolution, the central nervous system, sexism, racism, economy, politics, arts, music, and even the future of society, are all covered with the tantra yogic perspective of self-realization and the welfare of the universe.
